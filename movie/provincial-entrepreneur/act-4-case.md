# Act 4 — The Case

**Agents:** Chris + Priya
**Portals:** Consultant + Immigrant
**Duration:** Exploratory visit → net worth verification → business proposal → BC PNP registration → work permit

## Chapter 4.1 — Exploratory Visit

### Scene 4.1.1 — Planning the Trip
**Agent: Chris → Messages**
- "Priya, the exploratory visit isn't technically mandatory for the Base stream, but it's strongly recommended — and in my experience, applicants who skip it submit weaker proposals. I recommend 7-10 days in the Lower Mainland. Here's what we need to accomplish: 1) Visit 3-4 industrial spaces in Surrey for the garment facility. 2) Meet a local commercial realtor. 3) Meet a BC-based CPA for your net worth verification. 4) Visit the Surrey Board of Trade. 5) Document everything — photos, meeting notes, business cards. BC PNP will ask for a trip report."

### Scene 4.1.2 — Priya Visits BC
**Agent: Priya (narrated)**
- Flies Mumbai → Vancouver with Arjun picking her up at YVR
- Day 1-2: Tours Surrey industrial spaces with a commercial realtor
- Day 3: Meets BC-based CPA — begins net worth verification process
- Day 4: Visits Surrey Board of Trade, meets local garment industry contacts
- Day 5: Visits Simons distribution centre — they confirm interest in local sourcing
- Day 6: Meets a local lawyer about incorporating Mehta Garments Canada Inc
- Day 7: Explores Diya's potential school options in Surrey
- Takes photos, collects business cards, writes meeting notes
- Thinks: "Surrey makes sense. The industrial rents are reasonable. Simons wants local suppliers. Arjun is 40 minutes away. This can work."

### Scene 4.1.3 — Trip Report
**Agent: Chris**
- Priya sends photos, notes, meeting cards
- Chris drafts the exploratory visit report for BC PNP
- Includes: locations visited, business rationale for Surrey, contacts made, market observations
- Logs 4 hours → retainer balance: $11,000

## Chapter 4.2 — Net Worth Verification

### Scene 4.2.1 — CPA Engagement
**Agent: Priya**
- BC-based CPA (met during exploratory visit) begins independent net worth assessment
- Priya provides:
  - Mehta Textiles audited financial statements (3 years)
  - Property valuations (Mumbai apartment, Gujarat land)
  - Bank statements (6 months, India + UAE accounts)
  - Investment portfolio statements
  - Business valuation report from Indian CA

### Scene 4.2.2 — Verification Complete
**Agent: Chris**
- CPA issues net worth verification report: $812,000 CAD confirmed
- Exceeds $600K minimum
- Uploads to Priya's document checklist → "Verified"
- Logs 2 hours for coordination → retainer balance: $10,500

## Chapter 4.3 — Business Proposal

### Scene 4.3.1 — Chris Drafts the Proposal
**Agent: Chris**
- BC PNP requires a formal business proposal:
  - Executive summary
  - Business description (what Mehta Garments Canada will do)
  - Market analysis (Canadian sustainable fashion market)
  - Competitive advantage (existing supplier relationships)
  - Operations plan (Surrey facility, equipment, staffing)
  - Financial projections (3 years: revenue, costs, profit)
  - Job creation plan (8 positions: cutters, sewers, logistics, sales, admin)
  - Investment breakdown ($350K: lease, equipment, inventory, working capital)
  - Owner management plan (Priya = full-time Managing Director in BC)
- Chris uses Can'ArI to research market data and draft sections
- Reviews with Priya over 3 sessions via Messages
- Logs 12 hours → retainer balance: $7,500

### Scene 4.3.2 — Priya Reviews
**Agent: Priya**
- Reads the proposal carefully
- Pushes back on revenue projections: "Chris, $400K revenue in year 1 is too aggressive. I know this industry. Start with $250K. I'd rather underpromise and overdeliver — if I don't meet the target, I lose the nomination."
- Chris adjusts: "You're right. Conservative is smart for performance agreements."
- Thinks: "He listened. He didn't push back with pretty numbers. This is a consultant who understands risk."

## Chapter 4.4 — BC PNP Registration

### Scene 4.4.1 — Registration Submission
**Agent: Chris**
- Compiles the full BC PNP Entrepreneur registration package:
  - Personal information forms
  - Net worth verification report
  - Business proposal
  - Exploratory visit report
  - Language test results (IELTS General — CLB 7)
  - Education credentials
  - Business ownership proof (15 years)
- Submits to BC PNP online portal
- Case status: Intake → Submitted

### Scene 4.4.2 — BC PNP Interview
**Agent: Priya + Chris (narrated)**
- BC PNP schedules an interview (video call)
- Priya presents her business plan
- BC PNP officer asks about job creation timeline, market analysis, personal involvement
- Chris has prepped Priya on every likely question
- Interview goes well — officer notes the existing Simons/Aritzia relationships

### Scene 4.4.3 — Invitation to Apply
**Agent: Chris**
- BC PNP issues Invitation to Apply — business proposal approved
- Performance agreement terms: invest $350K, create 8 jobs, active management for 20 months
- Chris messages Priya: "Your proposal is approved. BC PNP has invited you to apply. There's also a $3,500 non-refundable processing fee to BC PNP when we submit the full application. Now we apply for a work permit to get you to Canada."
- Logs 3 hours → retainer balance: $6,750

## Chapter 4.5 — Work Permit

### Scene 4.5.1 — Work Permit Application
**Agent: Chris**
- Prepares federal work permit application based on BC PNP approval
- Includes: LMIA exemption (C11 entrepreneur), BC PNP invitation letter, business proposal
- Submits to IRCC
- Also prepares Diya's study permit application (tied to Priya's work permit)

### Scene 4.5.2 — Biometrics + Medical
**Agent: Priya**
- Completes biometrics at VFS Mumbai — Priya, Vikram (even though staying initially), Diya
- Medical exam at panel physician in Mumbai
- Uploads confirmations to portal

### Scene 4.5.3 — Work Permit Approved
**Agent: Chris**
- IRCC approves work permit: 2 years, employer-specific (Mehta Garments Canada)
- Diya's study permit: approved
- Chris messages: "Work permit approved. You and Diya can enter Canada. The performance clock starts when you begin business operations in Surrey."
- Retainer balance: $5,250
