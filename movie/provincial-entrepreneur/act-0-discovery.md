# Act 0 — Discovery

**Agent:** Priya Mehta
**Location:** Mumbai, India
**Duration:** From her son's phone call to "I need a consultant"

---

## Chapter 0.1 — The Call from Arjun

Priya's son Arjun calls from UBC. He's finishing his second year in Business. "Mom, my professor mentioned the Provincial Entrepreneur program. You could open the Canada factory you've been talking about AND get PR for the family. Look into it."

Priya opens her laptop that evening.

### Scene 0.1.1 — Research
- Priya googles "BC Provincial Entrepreneur immigration"
- Reads BC PNP website — confusing government language
- Finds CanaDREAMERS in the results
- Clicks through

### Scene 0.1.2 — Landing Page
- Sees "Stop guessing. Start belonging."
- Scrolls — sees consultant directory, eligibility tools
- Thinks: "Another immigration platform. But let me check if they understand business immigration — not just students and workers."

## Chapter 0.2 — Eligibility Check

### Scene 0.2.1 — Can'ArI Chat
- Clicks "Ask Can'ArI"
- Types: "I own a textile manufacturing company in India with 85 employees. I want to open a garment factory in Surrey, BC. My son is already studying at UBC. What immigration program fits me?"
- Can'ArI responds: BC Provincial Entrepreneur stream — requires $600K net worth, 3+ years business experience, business proposal, performance agreement. Exploratory visit strongly recommended.
- Thinks: "It knows the specific requirements. Net worth $600K — I have $800K. And it mentioned the exploratory visit. This is detailed."

### Scene 0.2.2 — Eligibility Assessment
- Enters: entrepreneur, textile/manufacturing, 15 years experience, $800K net worth, $350K investment planned, 8 jobs
- System shows: BC Provincial Entrepreneur — Strong Candidate
- Also flags: "Exploratory visit to BC strongly recommended before application"
- Thinks: "Strong candidate. But I need someone who's done this before. This is $350,000 of my family's money."

## Chapter 0.3 — Finding Chris

### Scene 0.3.1 — Consultant Directory
- Filters: specialization = "Provincial Entrepreneur"
- Chris Daw appears — RCIC-IRB L3, Vancouver, 20 years
- Specializations include both Provincial Entrepreneur AND Start-Up Visa
- Fee range: $8,000–20,000
- Thinks: "Vancouver-based. He'd know the BC program specifically. And $8,000-20,000 — expensive but this is a $350K investment. I need it done right."

### Scene 0.3.2 — Chris's Profile
- Reads Chris's bio — business immigration specialist
- Sees CICC Verified badge
- Sees the verification certificate — Entitled to Practice, RCIC-IRB L3
- Thinks: "Verified by the regulator. Not self-declared. Good. Let me sign up and talk to him."
