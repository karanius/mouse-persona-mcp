# Act 0 — Discovery

**Agent:** Ahmed Al-Rashid
**Portal:** Public website (localhost:3000)
**Duration:** Ahmed's first visit — from Google to "I want this consultant"

---

## Chapter 0.1 — The Search

Ahmed is in Dubai. His AgTech startup NovaTech has an MVP and 3 pilot farms. He's been thinking about Canada for months — the agricultural tech ecosystem, government grants, stability for his family. Tonight he opens his laptop.

### Scene 0.1.1 — Landing Page
- Ahmed googles "Canada startup visa consultant"
- Finds CanaDREAMERS
- Lands on homepage
- Sees: "Stop guessing. Start belonging."
- Thinks: "Bold. But do they understand business immigration? Most platforms are for Express Entry workers."

### Scene 0.1.2 — Exploring the Platform
- Scrolls through the homepage
- Sees consultant directory link
- Sees CanaVerse (explore Canada)
- Sees eligibility tools
- Thinks: "They have a consultant directory. That's different — most platforms don't connect you to a real RCIC."

## Chapter 0.2 — Eligibility Check

### Scene 0.2.1 — Can'ArI Chat
- Ahmed clicks "Ask Can'ArI"
- Types: "I'm a tech founder in Dubai. I want to bring my AI startup to Canada. What are my options?"
- Can'ArI responds with Start-Up Visa overview, requirements, designated organizations
- Thinks: "It knows about designated organizations. This isn't a generic chatbot."

### Scene 0.2.2 — Eligibility Assessment
- Ahmed clicks through to eligibility tool
- Enters: entrepreneur, tech sector, $200K funds, MSc CS, IELTS 7.5
- System shows: Start-Up Visa — Strong Candidate
- Also shows: Provincial Entrepreneur — Alternative Path
- Thinks: "Two options. I need a consultant who knows both."

## Chapter 0.3 — Finding Chris

### Scene 0.3.1 — Consultant Directory
- Ahmed navigates to the consultant directory
- Filters: specialization = "Start-Up Visa"
- Sees Chris Daw — RCIC-IRB L3, R409583, Vancouver
- Green CICC Verified badge
- 20 years experience
- Specializations: Start-Up Visa, Provincial Entrepreneur, PNP, FSWP
- Fee range: $10,000–25,000
- Thinks: "Verified by the actual regulatory body. Not self-declared. And he does both Start-Up Visa AND Provincial Entrepreneur — that's exactly what I need."

### Scene 0.3.2 — Chris's Public Profile
- Ahmed clicks Chris's name
- Sees the full profile card: company, bio, languages (English + French), city (Vancouver)
- Sees the CICC Verification Certificate (same one Chris sees in his dashboard)
- Reads Chris's bio about business immigration
- Thinks: "20 years. IRB authorization. Vancouver — that's where my UBC connection is. This is the one."

### Scene 0.3.3 — The Decision
- Ahmed sees "Book Consultation" button
- Hesitates — $10,000-25,000 is serious money
- Reads reviews (if any)
- Decides to sign up first, then book
- Clicks "Sign Up"
