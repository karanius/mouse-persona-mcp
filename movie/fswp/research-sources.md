## Real Stories That Inspired the Character

**Josh (India → Vancouver)** — HP senior manager with 120 employees. Landed in Vancouver, spent 5-6 months jobless. Flipped burgers and did data entry. Wife pregnant with twins — twice. Lived in single-room apartment. Eventually climbed back to supervisory role. Wife scored 100% on Canadian certification exams. The hardest immigration story.

**Alok (India/USA → Canada)** — 15-year IT professional (Data Warehouse, Cognos). Pre-networked heavily before arrival. Got multiple offers in 3 weeks including Deloitte and Royal Bank of Canada. Maintained same salary. Went from 14-16 hour workdays to 8-hour days. The strategic immigration story.

**Arjun (India → Canada)** — Renewable energy engineer. Used FSWP. High CRS score + job offer from research institute. The clean path story.

**Explorer101** — Lost right leg in accident DURING the application process. Climbed stairs 15-20 times a day to prove fitness for medical exam. Approved. The resilience story.
